# Faucet massa

### Explanation

A faucet in the context of a blockchain domain refers to a mechanism that distributes small amounts of cryptocurrency to users for free. It acts as a digital faucet, allowing individuals to obtain a small portion of a specific cryptocurrency without the need for extensive mining or purchasing. Faucets serve multiple purposes within the blockchain ecosystem. They act as a means to introduce new users to cryptocurrencies by providing them with a small amount to get started. Additionally, faucets can facilitate the testing and development of blockchain applications by offering developers a way to acquire the necessary tokens for experimentation.

### Requirements

- Have already a Massa wallet
- Faucet allows for one request per day, ensuring fair distribution of rewards.
- Have a [discord account](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360033931551-Getting-Started)

### How receive coins?

Sending your address to [testnet-faucet discord channel](https://discord.com/channels/828270821042159636/866190913030193172)
